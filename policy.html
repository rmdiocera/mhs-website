Admission Policy

Requirements Needed for Admission

  • · 1 x 1 Picture
  • · NSO Birth Certificate
  • · Baptismal Certificate
  • · F-138 (Report Card from previous school)
  • · Good Moral Character Certificate
  • · SSS ID
  • · Philhealth ID
  • · Mineski Membership Card
  • · Discount Card
  • · High Card

Additional Requirements for Senior High School

  • · For ESC Grantee, submit the ESC certificate.
  • · For Voucher Recipient, (from Public School), submit the Certificate of Completion (photocopy).
  • · For Voucher Recipient, (from Public School), submit the Certificate of Completion (photocopy).

Guidelines for Admission

1. Graduates of Makati High School are exempted from taking the entrance examination for high school but are required to submit the original Report Card on or before the specified date.
